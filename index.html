Bio

I graduated with a B.A. in Psychology from Lewis & Clark College in 2015. I initially went into college to become a clinical psychologist. When I realized that may not be the path for me, by chance and because of my love for math, my peers recommended that I try taking a CS course. I took my first computer programming class ever as a senior and by chance, fell in love, realizing I also had a natural knack for it. I discovered that I could still pursue my dream of influencing health on a societal level by pursuing apps and companies focused on health and wellness.
Since then, I've committed most of my time and energy outside of my 9-to-5 to full-stack web development, and I haven't looked back! When not online, I can be found painting, dog walking, at the gym, hiking, meditating, or exploring the city with my family.
